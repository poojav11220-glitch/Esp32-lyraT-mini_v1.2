What is the DS1302 RTC Module?

The DS1302 does NOT detect the actual (real-world) time by itself. It is essentially a very accurate clock/counter that counts time from an initial value.

What the Module Does — Step by Step

1. ESP32 powers ON
        │
        ▼
2. ESP32 reads DS1302 via CLK/DAT/RST wires
        │
        ▼
3. DS1302 sends back: seconds, minutes, hours, date, month, year
        │
        ▼
4. ESP32 prints it to serial monitor
        │
        ▼
5. ESP32 powers OFF  ←── battery takes over
        │
        ▼
6. DS1302 keeps counting silently (coin battery)
        │
        ▼
7. ESP32 powers ON again → reads correct updated time
